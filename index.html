About MediCare

Our Story of Care & Excellence

For over 25 years, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional healthcare services with compassion, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our patients' wellbeing.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, patient-centered healthcare services that promote healing, wellness, and quality of life for every individual we serve, while advancing medical excellence through continuous innovation and compassionate care.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in our region, recognized for medical excellence, innovative treatments, and exceptional patient experiences that set the standard for compassionate, quality healthcare delivery.

Our Journey Through Time

1998

Foundation

MediCare was founded with a vision to provide accessible, quality healthcare to our community. Started with 20 beds and 15 dedicated staff members.

Milestone: First 1,000 patients treated
2005

First Expansion

Added cardiac care unit and expanded to 100 beds. Introduced advanced diagnostic equipment and specialized treatment facilities.

Achievement: JCI Accreditation Received
2012

Technology Integration

Implemented electronic health records and telemedicine services. Became the first hospital in the region to offer robotic surgery.

Innovation: First Robotic Surgery Performed
2018

Community Programs

Launched free health screening programs and mobile clinic services. Established partnerships with local schools for health education.

Impact: 10,000+ free screenings conducted
2023

Modern Excellence

Achieved Joint Commission accreditation and expanded to 200 beds. Now serving over 50,000 patients annually with 150+ medical professionals.

Recognition: Regional Healthcare Excellence Award
